About The Red Cross

The Red Cross provides emergency assistance, disaster relief, and education in communities around the world. They help people prepare for and respond to emergencies, including natural disasters, conflicts, and health crises. Their work is vital in ensuring safety and humanitarian aid when disaster strikes.
